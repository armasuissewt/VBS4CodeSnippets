## Adjustable Camera Sensor

Here you find a complete vehicle model project for VBS4 25.2 defining an adjustable camera sensor [armasuisse.zip](https://github.com/armasuissewt/VBS4CodeSnippets/blob/main/rsc/armasuisse.zip).
There are custom changes in the config.cpp and model.cfg files, thus generating these with the MEX will brake the model.
